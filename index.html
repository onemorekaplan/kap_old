Security Customer Success Manager @ Akamai Technologies

I'm a technophile by day and a fitness fanatic by night. To keep my technology hunger satisfied, I protect all North American accounts that subscribe to Akamai's managed security service from modern web application and Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks. At night, boxing at my local gym keeps me in shape and at the top of my game. On the weekends, I like to relax and unwind by playing guitar and listening to music.

After working in Philadelphia as a consultant for 2 years, I moved to San Francisco to pursue a life in tech. In April of 2012, I undertook a new adventure by moving to Chicago. All has come full circle now as I just made my final move in June of 2014 back to where it all started: New York. As a native, I always knew I'd be back.
